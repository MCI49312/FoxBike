# FoxBike 🦊🚲

FoxBike is a lightweight, private, and feature-rich cycling computer for Android. Designed for cyclists who value their privacy, it provides real-time tracking, offline maps, and essential safety tools without any data leaving your device.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 📊 Real-Time Tracking
- **High-Precision Speedometer**: Real-time speed display based on GPS data.
- **Odometer**: Persistent total distance tracking that stays on your device across app restarts.
- **Comprehensive Session Stats**: View your current trip distance, average speed, maximum speed, and total elevation gain.
- **Stopwatch**: Precise measurement of your active riding time.
- **Calorie Calculator**: Estimated energy expenditure based on speed, duration, and customizable user weight.

### 🗺️ Mapping & Navigation
- **Local Maps (Beta)**: Powered by OpenStreetMap (osmdroid) for a private mapping experience.
- **Route Visualization**: Your path is drawn live on the map as you ride.
- **No Telemetry**: Maps are cached locally; no tracking data is sent to external servers.

### 🛡️ Safety & Tools
- **Service Reminder**: Customizable notifications (distance-based) to remind you about chain oiling or general bike maintenance.
- **Voice Feedback**: Periodic audio announcements of your distance and speed so you can keep your eyes on the road.

### ⚙️ Customization
- **Multi-Language Support**: Fully translated into English, Hungarian, German, Romanian, Polish, Bulgarian, Greek, Slovak, Czech, Chinese, Japanese, Serbian, and Croatian.
- **Dual Unit Support**: Switch easily between Metric (km/h, km, meters) and Imperial (mph, miles, feet).
- **Manual Dark Mode**: Force a dark UI for night rides regardless of system settings.
- **GPX Export**: Export your recorded tracks to standard GPX files for use in other applications like Strava.

## 🔒 Privacy First
- **No Telemetry**: We do not collect any usage data.
- **No Logs**: Your location history is never saved in a log file.
- **Offline Capable**: The app is designed to work without an internet connection (maps require initial caching).

## 🛠️ Technical Details
- **Platform**: Android (minSdk 24)
- **Language**: Kotlin
- **Map Provider**: OpenStreetMap via osmdroid
- **Location Services**: Google Play Services Location
